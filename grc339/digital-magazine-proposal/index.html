Introduction

Die Wanderin

"Die Wanderin," translated "The Wanderer" (feminine) in German, is a travel magazine. From the perspective of a wandering 20-something student learning about the world around her as she tries to find her place in it, "Die Wanderin" features articles on the various cities and towns she passes through.
